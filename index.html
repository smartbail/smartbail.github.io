A social enterprise

SmartBail is a social enterprise with the mission to make Canada's criminal justice system more efficient, effective and equitable. Research suggests that SmartBail can decrease the number of defendants, including the percentage of racialized defendants, in pre-trial detention without affecting the crime rate. This saves the government money, results in fewer legally innocent defendants in remand, and increases equity in the Canadian bail system.

Accurate Risk Assessment

SmartBail creates accurate risk assessment profiles of defendants going through the Canadian bail system. It is a machine learning technology that assesses whether defendants will attend their court hearings or be charged with new criminal offences if they are released on bail. It creates these assessments using variables that comply with the Canadian Charter of Rights and Freedoms.

Legal Innovation Zone

SmartBail is a part of the Legal Innovation Zone at Ryerson University, the world's first legal technology incubator. It is also a part of the competitive JumpStart Program with Waterloo's Accelerator Centre.
