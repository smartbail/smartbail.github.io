Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy matters a lot to us and therefore we have developed this Privacy Policy so that you can better understand how we collect, use and disclose your personal information. This Privacy Policy applies to the Madiba Technologies Inc. ("SmartBail") website located at www.smartbail.ca and all associated sites and platforms linked to www.smartbail.ca by SmartBail (collectively, the "Site"), which provide information, data, texts, graphics and other materials (collectively, the "Content"), regardless of how you access or use the Site, including through mobile devices. By using the Site, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy and, where applicable, our Terms of Use.

2. What information do we collect and why?

  • When you communicate with us, we may collect information from you such as your name, contact information and other details.
  • We use this information to contact you and to improve our service and maintain a relationship.

3. How do we protect your information?

SmartBail takes the security of your personal information very seriously, but no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information. However, we do take serious measures to minimize risks:

  • Our website is scanned on a regular basis for security holes and known vulnerabilities.
  • We implement a number of security measures such as traffic encryption using Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology.
  • In general, we have taken certain physical, administrative, and technical steps to safeguard the information that we collect from you.

4. How long do we retain your information?

In general, we keep your information collected through the Site for a reasonable period of time for business-related reasons. The specific retention times can vary but you may request to delete your information with us at any time (see Section 7 listed below)

5. When and why do we share your personal information?

SmartBail highly respects the confidentiality of your information, and we do not sell or trade your personal information without your prior consent. However, in the following situations, we may disclose your personal information to third parties:

  • Where required by law enforcement or other legal proceedings, we may disclose your information to respond to lawful court orders or to conform to legal requirements.
